Combat

  • Aim (A-X)
    Looks for aim that turns too cleanly or snaps too hard.
  • Bow Aim (A)
    Looks for bow aim that stays too clean or locks too fast.
  • Auto Clicker (A-T)
    Looks for click patterns that are too steady or too repeated.
  • Auto Block (A-F)
    Looks for blocking and hitting at times that should not happen together.
  • Kill Aura (A-G)
    Looks for attack patterns that do not fit normal player input.
  • Crystal Aura (A-C)
    Looks for crystal actions that are too fast or too clean.
  • Reach (A-B)
    Looks for hits from farther away than they should be.
  • Container Reach (A)
    Looks for opening containers from too far away.
  • HitBox (A)
    Checks if the hit really lines up with the target.
  • WTap (A)
    Looks for repeated sprint resets around hits.
  • AutoTotem (A)
    Looks for totem swaps that happen too fast.
  • Backtrack (A-B)
    Looks for hits on old target positions instead of the real one.
  • Combat Engine (A)
    Builds a score over time from how someone fights.
  • Crystal Engine (A)
    Builds a score over time from crystal fights.

  • Prediction (A)
    Checks if movement matches normal vanilla physics.

    Looks for staying in the air longer than normal.

    Looks for elytra movement that is too fast or too controlled.
  • Vehicle Fly (A-C)
    Looks for boats and mounts leaving normal movement.
  • Player Speed (A-E)
    Looks for moving faster than normal for the situation.
  • Vehicle Speed (A)
    Looks for vehicles going faster than they should.
  • Invalid (A-O)
    Looks for movement states and values that do not make sense.
  • Invalid Vehicle (A-F)

  • Jesus (A-F)
    Looks for walking on water in ways vanilla does not allow.
  • Sprint (A-F)
    Looks for sprint changes that should not be possible.
  • NoFall (A-C)
    Looks for fake ground and missing fall damage.
  • NoWeb (A-B)
    Looks for moving through webs without the normal slowdown.
  • Step (A-B)
    Looks for stepping up higher than vanilla allows.
  • GroundSpoof (A)
    Looks for saying you are on ground while still in the air.
  • Strafe (A)
    Looks for movement control that is too strong.
  • NoSlow (A)
    Looks for missing slowdown while using items.
  • NoSaddle (A)
    Looks for ride behavior that breaks normal saddle rules.
  • FastLadder (A)
    Looks for climbing faster than ladders or vines should allow.
  • AntiLevitation (A)
    Looks for players ignoring levitation.
